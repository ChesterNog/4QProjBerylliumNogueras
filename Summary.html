Basic Information on Possible Symptoms

Cough

Your airways, stomach or esophagus are irritated or blocked, causing the body to force it out by forcing muscles and air. Make sure to drink water and identify if there are foreign objects within the three.

Fever

Your immune system is responding to something within your body. Drink some water and rest well, fevers generally last a few days.

Shortness of Breath

Your airways are either inflamed or blocked, medication is advised.

Fatigue

Fatigue is generally caused by excessive physical or mental exertion, you should rest for a while.

Nausea

Can be caused by anything from stress to poisoning, if rest does not relieve nausea after an hour or two, seek medical attention.
